Why Choose Mackenzie?

With over a decade of hands-on experience, I began my journey in my family’s painting business and later established my own. From iconic local spots like L Street Tavern and Wing-Itz in Hampton, NH, to charming boutique hotels like the Essex Street Inn in Newburyport and Mountain Edge Suites in Newbury, NH—I’ve brought life and personality to hundreds of spaces.

I take immense pride in my work. Every detail matters, and every client is treated like a neighbor—because often, they are. My reputation is built on word of mouth, quality finishes, and never leaving a job until the customer is thrilled.
