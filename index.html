Why Choose Us

Mackenzie Doherty is a second-generation painter with over a decade of hands-on experience. After seven years working in her family’s painting business, she launched her own company, quickly becoming a trusted name in the region. Her portfolio includes L Street Tavern and Wing Itz in Hampton, NH; Essex Street Inn in Newburyport, MA; and Mountain Edge Suites in Newbury, NH. Mackenzie’s commitment to quality and customer satisfaction means your project will be treated like it’s her own home.
