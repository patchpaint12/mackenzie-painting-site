Craftsmanship with Care

Mackenzie Doherty is the owner and lead painter of Doherty Patch & Paint, a proudly female-owned painting company serving Southern New Hampshire and the North Shore of Massachusetts. With over a decade of experience, Mackenzie brings precision, care, and a deep knowledge of both traditional and modern painting techniques to every project.

Her portfolio ranges from single-room refreshes to large-scale commercial properties, including boutique hotels and full-home renovations. Whether it’s a kitchen cabinet refinishing or a complete interior overhaul, Mackenzie approaches each space with the same commitment: to make it beautiful, lasting, and uniquely yours.

  • Exceptional attention to detail
  • High-end finishes without pretension
  • Respect for your space, schedule, and goals
