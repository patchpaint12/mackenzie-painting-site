Hi, I’m Mackenzie.

I’ve been painting homes and businesses for over 10 years across southern New Hampshire and the North Shore of Massachusetts. What began as a summer job quickly turned into a craft I love — and a business built on trust, detail, and pride in every finish.

Whether it’s a crisp new coat in a coastal kitchen or refreshing an old inn, I bring the same care to every brushstroke. I believe painting is not just about color — it’s about creating calm, clean spaces where people feel at home.

Thanks for visiting — I’d love to hear about your project!
