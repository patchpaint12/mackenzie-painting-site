About Mackenzie

Mackenzie Doherty is a professional painter with over 10 years of hands-on experience. Based in southern New Hampshire, she specializes in both residential and commercial painting throughout the Seacoast and the North Shore of Massachusetts.

What sets Mackenzie apart is her commitment to detail, her deep respect for historic New England homes, and her belief that great painting work should feel effortless, timeless, and lasting.

Whether it’s restoring trim on a 19th-century colonial or refreshing a coastal storefront, Mackenzie brings a personal, thoughtful touch to every project.
