Why Choose Us

I’m Mackenzie, a painter with over a decade of hands-on experience. I began in my family’s painting business, and three years ago, I launched my own. My work spans from cherished Seacoast favorites like L Street Tavern and Wing Itz in Hampton, NH to hospitality landmarks like the Essex Street Inn in Newburyport, MA and Mountain Edge Suites in Newbury, NH. I approach each project with pride, professionalism, and an unwavering commitment to quality. Chances are, I've painted a home or business you know — and I’ve never left a client unsatisfied.

Our Services

From historic home restorations and cabinet refinishing to commercial interiors, coastal exteriors, and detailed trimwork — we bring color and craftsmanship to every corner.
